Hi There!

This is a B2B client relations management app I made to showcase some of my angular-5 skills. The App uses latest editions.

Features
  • Mean stack (mongo, express, angular, node)
  • JWT sessions & validation
  • Mongoose schemas
  • A debounce function (lazy loading)
  • AWS S3 integration
  • Flexobx layout

Usage

Register as a new Company, and Login.

Or

Use the company username '8888', password '8888' to login right away.

Once logged-in you can view your customers, send them invoices and redeem invoices you already sent.

You can also view all the other companies in the system, and subscribe as their customer. If you do so - they will be able to send you invoices.

Finally - you can view and pay for invoices sent to you by the companies you are customer of.
